Paragraph¶

Paragraphs contain text and may contain inline markup: emphasis, strong emphasis, interpreted text, inline literals, standalone hyperlinks (http://www.python.org), external hyperlinks (Python), internal cross-references (example_), footnote references (1), citation references ([CIT2002]), substitution references (|example|), and inline internal targets.

Paragraphs are separated by blank lines and are left-aligned. Adding the following will change the appearance of the above…
